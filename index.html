CARNIVAL 2020 RULES



RAFFLE DRAW


Event: Two random winners will see their ticket drawn before the end of the night. Raffle tickets can be earned throughout the carnival for the following activities:
- Showing up - 2 tickets
- RSVPing - 1 ticket
- Bringing a friend/stranger - 2 tickets per person
- Bringing a snack/beverage - 2 tickets
- Showing exceptional carnival spirit - 1 ticket

Prize: Two-dollar bill + an invite to the Carnival Championship Round.



PLUMP THE TRUMP


Event: Set your high score on Plump the Trump. Three highest scorers will be invited to a sudden death finals.



Prize: Plump the Trump embroidered tie + $10 donation to a presidential candidate of your choice + an invite to the Carnival Championship Round.






LET THERE BE LIGHT


Event: Set your high score on Let There be Light (Blast Mode). Three highest scorers will be invited to a sudden death finals.



Prize: Let There Be Light commemorative etched glassware + an invite to the Carnival Championship Round.





GUESS THE WEIGHT OF SAM RICHMAN AND TOM WADDICK


Event: Sam Richman and Tom Waddick will step on a scale. At the same time. How much do they weigh? Closest to the actual total wins.

Prize: A zucchini with your name carved into it + an invite to the Carnival Championship Round.





TULIP MANIA


Event: Set your high score on Tulip Mania. Three highest scorers will be invited to a sudden death finals.



Prize: Limited edition Tulip Mania T-Shirt + an invite to the Carnival Championship Round.





BLASTERS


Event: Set your high score on Blasters (Classic Mode). Three highest scorers will be invited to a sudden death finals.



Prize: Blasters Championship Hat + an invite to the Carnival Championship Round.






THE BLATT BOX



Event: Set your high score on The Blatt Box (Speed Mode). Three highest scorers will be invited to a sudden death finals.



Prize: The Blatt Box Beanie + an invite to the Carnival Championship Round.







CARNIVAL CHAMPIONSHIP ROUND


Event: A Surprise!

Prize: A priceless nine-pound faux delft tile novelty check for one-million dollars.
